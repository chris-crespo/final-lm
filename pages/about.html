About Us

kiddycamp was established in 1917 and is located on the beautiful 85-acre seaside campus of Tabor Academy. Our summer camp gives young people ages 6-17 the opportunity to develop their full potential as individuals, choosing how they want to spend their days at camp. Under the guidance of highly qualified counselors, the program encourages young people to take pride in their personal achievements in the classroom, on the playing fields, in the art studios, or on the waterfront. The Tabor Summer Camp provides the option of personal growth without losing any of the joys of summer.

KiddyCamp was founded for four mission piliars:
