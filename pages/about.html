It is our mission to Monitor and Guide littel Kids to devolope their Skills

kiddyCamp was established in 1917 and is located on the beautiful 85-acre seaside land. Our summer camp gives young people ages 4-16 the opportunity to develop their full potential as individuals, choosing how they want to spend their days at camp. Under the guidance of highly qualified counselors, the program encourages young people to take pride in their personal achievements in the camp, on the playing fields, in the art studios, or on the waterfront. The Tabor Summer Camp provides the option of personal growth without losing any of the joys of summer.

We Guide your childrens by our Values

We believe that every child has the potential to be a great person. We believe that every child has a Hidden Talent that they can use to make a difference in the world. so we want to help them to develop their skills and make a difference in the world. With our camp valunerable values.
